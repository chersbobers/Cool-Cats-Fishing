Play the Game

How to Play

  1. Cast your line: Click "Cast Line!" to start fishing
  2. Catch fish: When a fish bites, quickly press the shown key (A,S,D,F,J,K,L)
  3. Collect them all: Different fish types have varying rarities
  4. Redeem codes: Visit the Shop to unlock special bonuses
  5. Manage inventory: Check what you've caught and sell fish for profit

Fish Types

  • Common: Goldfish, Salmon (Easiest to catch)
  • Uncommon: Tuna, Mackerel
  • Rare: Swordfish, Pufferfish
  • Legendary: Shark, Mermaid's Hair (Very rare!)
  • Secret Fish: Unlock with special codes
